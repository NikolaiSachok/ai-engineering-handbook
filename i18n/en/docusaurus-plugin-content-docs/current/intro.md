# Enterprise RAG & Agents Handbook

🚧 **Work in progress.** This is a placeholder — content will be filled in separately.

A practical, first-principles guide to production RAG (retrieval-augmented generation)
and agentic systems.

## Who this handbook is for

The material is written for three audiences at once:

- **Learners** — any engineer who wants to understand RAG and agents from first principles,
  including the "why" and the failure modes, not just a feature list.
- **The author's own reference** — a durable record of design principles and decisions.
- **Portfolio** — a demonstration of engineering maturity (eval, guardrails, observability,
  design discipline).

## Structure

- **Part I — RAG:** ingestion, retrieval, generation, and cross-cutting concerns.
- **Part II — Agents:** agentic RAG, tool use, loops, planning, multi-agent.
- **Glossary:** single definitions for terms that other pages link to.
